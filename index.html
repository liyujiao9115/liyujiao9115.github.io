The Most Beautiful Thing

If everything in the world is up for sale, time will become the most expensive and beautiful one.
The wisdom way to cherish time is to remember how much you have got instead of how much you have lost.
时间是世界上最珍贵最美丽的事物,
不要在意有多少过去已经逝去，只需牢记有多少曾经已然走过。
